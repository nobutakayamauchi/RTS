Who this is for

  • You can “build with AI”, but reproducibility collapsed: you can’t explain why it worked yesterday.
  • You’re shipping under responsibility (work, clients, team) and need defensible decisions.
  • Your AI workflow became a tangled graph (branches) and you need a single runnable line.
  • You want to restore execution with provenance — not more tools.

Who this is NOT for

  • If you want me to “build everything” or run multiple parallel branches for a fixed fee.
  • If you need live meetings as default. (Zoom is optional add-on.)
  • If you can’t share logs / artifacts at all. (RTS needs evidence.)

What you get (S Plan)

  • Structural audit of your current workflow/logs (where reproducibility breaks).
  • Reconstruction: what happened, what was decided, what was missing.
  • One-route design: select the most valuable line and make it runnable.
  • RTS deliverables:
    • Decision Log (D#)
    • Information Environment Log (InfoEnv)
    • Risk/Safety Log
    • Next 7-day execution map
  • Q&A via async messages for a limited window (see scope).

Scope boundaries (important)

  • This is not multi-branch “everything rescue”. One route only.
  • If your project is a “bundle of routes” (stakeholders / network / external vendors), it may be classified as Corporate scope.
  • If scope expands after audit, I will tell you early. You can stop without pressure.

FAQ

Q: Can you guarantee results?
A: No. What I guarantee is structural clarity, a runnable route, and a log-based reconstruction that improves reproducibility and decision defensibility.

Q: Is this consulting?
A: It’s closer to external audit + reconstruction. Less talk, more deliverables.

Q: Why no Zoom by default?
A: Live sessions create instant-answer pressure and reduce audit quality. Async produces better structure.

Q: Can a company apply?
A: Yes, but corporate scope typically starts higher. If it’s “personal but actually a company machine”, I will classify it as corporate for fairness and workload control.
