Why RTS?

Modern AI workflows restart thinking every session. Ideas disappear. Decisions fragment. Execution history is lost. RTS preserves reasoning continuity across AI sessions.

Built for AI Operators

  • AI researchers running long experiments
  • GPT workflow builders
  • Startup operators
  • Prompt / agent engineers
  • Anyone treating AI execution as infrastructure

What RTS Does

  • Captures execution history
  • Preserves reasoning decisions
  • Freezes incidents (hash + archive)
  • Separates evidence from analysis
  • Enables rollback and audit trails
